Privacy Policy

Puja Patel LLC · puja-patel.com · Last updated: July 16, 2026

This page explains what information is collected when you visit puja-patel.com and how it's used. The short version: this site collects no personal information directly — no forms, no accounts. It uses two measurement tools: an analytics tool (Microsoft Clarity) to understand how visitors use the page, and an advertising pixel (Meta) to measure where visitors come from.

Analytics & session recordings

This site uses Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with the page — where they scroll, what they click, and where they get stuck. Clarity captures behavioral data such as mouse movement, scrolling, clicks/taps, pages viewed, device and browser type, and approximate location (from your IP address), and may replay anonymized sessions. Clarity uses first- and third-party cookies and similar technologies to do this.

We use this data to improve the site and its content. Data captured by Clarity is processed under Microsoft's Privacy Statement. You can learn more about Clarity at clarity.microsoft.com.

Advertising measurement (Meta Pixel)

This site also uses the Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram) to measure which of our posts and campaigns bring visitors here, and to enable Meta to show our content to similar audiences. The pixel shares standard event data with Meta — pages viewed, buttons clicked (such as starting checkout), device and browser information, and identifiers from Meta cookies (such as _fbp) — which Meta processes under Meta's Privacy Policy.

You can control how Meta uses this data via Meta Ad Preferences, and opt out of interest-based advertising generally at optout.aboutads.info.

What this site does NOT collect

  • No contact forms, sign-ups, or accounts on this site
  • No payment information — ever — on this site
  • No sale of personal information to anyone

Checkout & membership

If you join The Second Brain Method, checkout happens on Circle (circle.so), our community platform, with payments processed by their payment provider. Any information you enter there (name, email, payment details) is collected and processed by Circle under Circle's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We receive your name and email as part of your membership; we never see or store your full payment details.

Cookies

Cookies on this site are set by the two tools described above: Microsoft Clarity (analytics) and the Meta Pixel (advertising measurement, e.g. _fbp). The site also stores the campaign source of your first visit (UTM parameters) in your browser's local storage to understand which content brought you here. You can block or delete cookies and site data in your browser settings; the site works fine without them.

Your choices

  • Opt out of analytics: most browsers offer tracking prevention, private browsing, or extensions that block analytics scripts — Clarity respects these.
  • Data questions or deletion requests: email us (below) and we'll handle it, including relaying deletion requests to Microsoft, Meta, or Circle where applicable.

Children

This site is for business owners and is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the update will appear on this page with a new "last updated" date.
